SDGolf Monitor — Privacy policy

Effective July 10, 2026

SDGolf Monitor is a small, invite-only tee-time alert service for San Diego golf courses, run personally by its maintainer for a group of friends. It watches course booking sites and notifies you — by email and, if you opt in, by text message — when a tee time matching your saved search appears. It sells nothing and shows no ads.

What we collect

  • Account email and password (password stored as a salted, peppered hash — never in plain text).
  • Your alert subscriptions: courses, dates, times, and player filters you set up, plus any "away" dates you mark.
  • If you opt in to text alerts: your mobile phone number and a consent record — the timestamp, IP address, browser user-agent, and the exact version of the consent language you agreed to — plus a log of verification and opt-out events.
  • Bug reports you choose to submit, including the description you type and recent client-side error logs.

How it's used

For exactly one purpose: sending you the tee-time alerts you asked for and operating the service (signing you in, filing your bug reports, keeping legally required consent records). All messages — email and SMS — are informational alerts you requested. We send no marketing or promotional messages of any kind.

Text messages (SMS)

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Phone numbers and SMS opt-in data are never sold, rented, or shared with anyone for marketing.

  • Text alerts are strictly opt-in: you enter your own number, agree to the disclosure, and confirm a one-time verification code before any alert is sent.
  • Message frequency varies with tee-time availability. Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
  • Reply STOP to any message to cancel immediately. Reply HELP for help, or contact the email below.
  • The complete opt-in workflow — including the enrollment form, the exact consent language, and sample messages — is documented publicly at /sms-optin.

Who processes your data

  • Cloudflare — hosts this site and stores account and subscription data.
  • Twilio — delivers text messages (they receive your phone number and the message content).
  • GitHub — runs the monitoring job.
  • Google (Gmail) — delivers alert emails.

No analytics, ad networks, or data brokers. Data is never shared beyond the processors above, except if required by law.

Retention

  • SMS consent and opt-out records are retained for at least 5 years, including after you opt out or your account is deleted — this is our record that you asked to receive texts.
  • The lookup entry that makes your number routable for alerts is deleted the moment you opt out (by STOP, by the UI toggle, or by account removal) — after that, nothing can text you even by accident.
  • Account and subscription data is deleted when your account is removed.

Your choices

  • Turn text alerts off any time in the app ("Texts") or by replying STOP.
  • Unsubscribe from any alert email with its one-click unsubscribe link.
  • Ask for your account or data to be removed by emailing the address below.

Questions, help, or data requests: sdgolfmonitor@gmail.com
